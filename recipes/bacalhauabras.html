Bacalhau à Brás

Is a typical portuguese dish, amazing flavour a mix of potatoes with eggs and the fish called Bacalhau from Norway

Ingredients

  • fried potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • Bacalhau

Steps

  1. first cook the bacalhau
  2. then add the potatoes
  3. then add the eggs
  4. and at the end you can choose to add some green flavour to it, like salsa.

Prego no prato

Is another typical portuguese meal, this time with the classic steak, chips and egg.

Ingredients

  • cow steak
  • 1 egg
  • rice
  1. cook things separately, start with the rice
  2. then fry some potatoes
  3. then do the steak, followed by the egg
  4. this way we ensure at the end everything is still hot
